PATRIOTIC LEAGUE OF UGANDA

The Patriotic League of Uganda (PLU) is a political organization in Uganda chaired by Gen. David Muhoozi Kainerugaba

Was established with aim of working toward reviving in ugandans a spirit of good citizenship, national pride, national service, protection of vulnerable persons, combating corruption and wastage of public resources, and protection of the environment
